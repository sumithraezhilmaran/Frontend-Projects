About Me

I'm Sumithra Ezhilmaran, a third-year M.Tech student in Computer Science & Engineering, with a focused specialisation in Cybersecurity. My academic journey has been driven by a single question: how do we build digital systems that are resilient, trustworthy, and secure by design?

My work spans network security, vulnerability assessment, cryptographic protocols, and ethical hacking. I'm currently working on my thesis around intrusion detection using machine learning, combining my interest in security with data-driven approaches.

Beyond research, I actively participate in Capture The Flag (CTF) competitions, which sharpen my practical offensive and defensive skills. I believe that understanding how attacks work is the foundation of building better defences.

I'm seeking opportunities in security research, red teaming, SOC analysis, or any role where rigour and curiosity are valued.
